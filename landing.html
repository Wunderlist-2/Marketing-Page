Vision

Wunderlist 2 is an application intended to solve one of lifes most fundamental conumdrums, scheduling our days and implementing our todo lists. We have an app for that! You can create recurring scheduled task, as well as easily add and remove items from your todo list. parents will no longer have need to worry about forget to pick up their young ones from school or after school activities. If you have ADHD like me, remembering important tasks can easily be forgotten when focusing on other things. With Wunderlist, you to can become a master procrastinator, and still get things done!
